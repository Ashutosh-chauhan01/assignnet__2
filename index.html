About the Scientist

I am the inventor of the World Wide Web, revolutionizing global communication and information sharing. He implemented the first successful communication between a Hypertext Transfer Protocol (HTTP) client and server via the Internet in 1989. Berners-Lee is a professor at MIT and the University of Oxford and continues to advocate for an open and accessible web. His work has earned him numerous accolades, including a knighthood in 2004.
